About Us

10joules wants to create an active community of homeschooling families with a common goal of providing quality home education to our generation’s young minds.

Our goal is to make homeschooling easier and more connected than ever before.

Through 10joules, homeschooling families can create lasting and meaningful relationships while providing the education their children deserve.
